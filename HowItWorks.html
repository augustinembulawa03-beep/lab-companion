Why this approach works

This instructional sequence is designed for progressive scaffolding and independent lab performance.

  • Reduces cognitive overload
  • Reinforces conceptual understanding
  • Supports independent lab work

Each lab follows the same step-by-step structure so that once you are familiar with the workflow in Lab 1, later labs become easier to navigate. This helps you focus more on the measurements and circuit behaviour rather than searching for instructions during the session.
